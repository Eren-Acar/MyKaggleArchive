Dataset Overview:
This dataset provides a comprehensive list of the world's tallest women and men, meticulously gathered from Wikipedia. It includes detailed information about each individual, such as their country of origin, height in both metric and imperial units, name, and notable facts. Additionally, the dataset includes lifespan information, highlighting the age at death where applicable. This collection offers valuable insights into the extraordinary heights achieved by individuals across different regions and times.

Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kanchana1990/worlds-tallest-men-and-women/data
